---

# Objective
Generate a complete kids YouTube Shorts video content.

# Steps

1. Create a catchy YouTube title
   - Keep it short and engaging
   - Add emoji if relevant

2. Generate a short story (50–100 words)
   - Use very simple English
   - Make it easy for children to understand
   - Include a clear moral lesson

3. Break the story into scenes
   - 5–8 short scenes
   - Each scene should describe visuals clearly
   - Keep scenes short (1–2 lines)

4. Thumbnail idea
   - Suggest a colorful, eye-catching thumbnail
   - Focus on main character and emotion

# Rules
- Content must be safe for kids
- No violence, fear, or adult themes
- Use friendly and positive tone
- Keep sentences short and simple

# Output Format

Title:
<video title>

Story:
<full story>

Scenes:
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...

Thumbnail Idea:
<description>
